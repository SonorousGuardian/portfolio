## üìò Overview
This project defines the **Information Security Policy** framework for *SBI Financials*, aligning with **ISO 27001** and **NIST CSF** principles.  
It establishes clear guidelines to ensure **confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA)** of information assets across the organization.

---

## üéØ Purpose
- Protect information assets from unauthorized access or misuse.  
- Define clear roles, responsibilities, and control ownership.  
- Support governance, risk management, and compliance (GRC) initiatives.  
- Promote a culture of security awareness and accountability.

---

## üìç Scope
Applies to all:
- Employees, contractors, and third-party vendors.  
- IT systems, networks, applications (including SaaS), and data.  
- Business units handling company or customer information.

---

## üîë Key Policy Areas
- **Information Classification:** Public / Internal / Confidential / Restricted  
- **Access Control:** Least privilege, MFA, periodic access reviews  
- **Vulnerability Management:** Regular scans and penetration testing  
- **Incident Response:** SOC-led detection, containment, and recovery  
- **Personnel Security:** Background checks and annual awareness training  
- **Physical Security:** Controlled access, CCTV, and fire protection
